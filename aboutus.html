ABOUT US

Quality cookware for quality meals

Every home cook needs a good quality set of pots and pans. And having a selection specifically based on your needs might even inspire you to spend more time preparing healthy, homemade meals. Win, win.

Discover our range of bowls, plates and dinnerware sets – whether you’re enjoying a morning bowl of cereal or cooking a romantic dinner for two - we have an option for every food occasion!. You will be spoilt for choices with our extensive collections of the most efficiently and cleverly designed tableware and kitchen accessories.

Ceramic dinner sets

It’s clear our ambiance-conscious generation appreciates the art of a well set table (a floral centerpiece, flickering tea lights, and a name card just begs for an Instagram!) yet few actually own the accoutrements to create their own.

This inimitable tableware skilfully etched by miniature painting artists from Rajasthan is full of rustic country kitchen charm. Bronze, copper and brass used in the set are not just pleasing to the eye but also boost immunity. What makes this range one-of-a-kind are the designs that range from enchanted, unmapped forests, intricate motifs of Mughal gardens, to the bright bougainvillea and Gulmohar trees that light up the streets. The imperfections and slight irregularities of the hand crafted pieces only add to the charm of the product. Combination of green and sage glaze with wood lends the collection a holistic appeal.
